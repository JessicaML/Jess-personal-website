Performance art

  • videoHELLO

    videoHELLO is a TV show from the future - Daniel is here to discover the world and protect the radical self-actualisation rights of children, animals and plants.

    • "...as impressive as it was barmy, as humorous as it was inexplicable."

    Slate the Disco

  • Suicide Sue and her Week or Wonders

    Suicide Sue suffers from depression, however, after confiding in a Librarian, she spends a week discovering the joys of web-based user-generated content. Entertainment made by and for normal people, and her world is transformed...

  • The Cybernetic Supermarket

    Martin and his mum go shopping at the supermarket. His mum has some trouble deciding which apples to buy and some strange things happen...

    • "...incredibly unique, ambitious and entertaining." "Blue Jammy Chris Morris-esque... enjoyably bonkers."

    London City Nights

  • Ellen Thornton's feminist Sci Fi Video-blog

    Ellen Thornton is my feminist sci fi-loving alter ego. She has taken a year off from her job at a local council to explore - the traditionally male preserve - science fiction. She helps us explore time, space and even our minds.
